Block Next

The future of Internet finance

Purpose

We provide a suite of financial services built on the internet of value. The products offered by Block Next will fulfill the banking needs of 90 percent of the world, specifically allowing the unbanked, underbanked, and the bottom of the pyramid to reach financial goals and participate in the global economic system.

Vision

Block Next aims to use technology to change the current polarized economy into a level playing field by empowering individuals from all over the world, and specifically developing countries, to participate in a global finance system.

By eliminating the need to have a bank account or specific technical equipment such as a computer, Block Next systems form the backbone of the next global economy. This revolutionary finance system will provide universal access to fair trade, fair exchange, and goods and services that previously, through existing banking systems, have be reserved for the economically advantaged.

In addition to preventing fraud through digital asset verification, Block Next helps prevent fraud by providing a reliable system of documenting all transactions. This is especially important in developing countries where a lack of infrastructure can lead to account and transaction tampering, a situation where the unbanked and underbanked are at distinct disadvantage.

Bank in the Box systems eliminate the need for personal computers to access global finance systems. They also serve as a way to educate and inform anyone who wants to know more about how the technology works or the philosophy behind it.
